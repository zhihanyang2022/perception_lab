Memory Experiment

Perception Lab @ Carleton

Instructions. You will be shown 10 words in a sequence, each lasting for 2 seconds. Meanwhile, a noise file will be played. After all words have been shown, you will be given an arbitrary long period to record the 10 words displayed to you in order - you can simply stop the recording after you've finished. In the end, you will be shown the correct answers. The experiment will start 3 seconds after you've clicked the "Begin Experiment" button below.

Warning. The noise file might be too loud given your computer's volume. Make sure to decrease your computer's volume before clicking the "Begin Experiment" button below.
